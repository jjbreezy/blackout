Blackout!: The Card Game

RULES

2+ Players

Ages 21 and up.

Contains explicit content

Gameplay Rules

Every player must finish a full glass of water over the course of a single game. Any team who has water remaining in any of their players' glasses will forefit.

A player may choose to skip their turn if they draw a card they are not comfortable with for any reason. A player who skips their turn will leave their piece unmoved.

There is no limit to the number of skips a player may use, but one should keep in mind that skipping your turn puts your crew at a significant disadvantage.

If any player vomits, the game is over immediately. The vomiting player's team forefits the game for allowing their teammate to exceed their limits.

Every turn, the player will pour an amount of their drink into the Black Hole Cup.

The player whose pour causes the glass to spill will finish the Black Hole Cup. That player does not move to the next wormhole.

Your Turn

Player will roll the dice once at the start of their turn (unless otherwise instructed by a card).

Player will move their piece the number of spaces displayed on the dice

Once their piece is finished moving, Player will draw a card from the deck unless they have landed on a special space

Player will choose to either act on the card they have drawn or to pass and forefit their turn.

If Player wishes to pass on a special space, player will return their piece to the space it was in before they moved.

Player may choose to go left or right towards their goal when they are faced with a crossroads.

Cards

There are many different kinds of cards in Blackout.

  • Spill: a confession of fact.
  • Dare: a dare to do an action.
  • Group: a question to the whole group.
  • Movement: how a player's piece will move.
  • Game: a game to be played with other players.
  • Rule: a new game rule, triggered by the action specified on the card. It helps to write these down.
  • Blackout: An eyes-closed blind vote where players in the minority will drink

Special Spaces

There are special spaces that trigger certain actions when they are landed on by a player

Wormhole

A player who lands in a wormhole will drink the entirety of the black hole cup in the center of the board.

That player will then move one wormhole closer to their goal side.

If no wormholes remain between the player and their goal landing point (side), that player moves directly to their landing point.

Starting the Game

The players will divide into two teams before the game begins, and sit alternating teams around the board.

The oldest player will play first, the turn order proceeds clockwise from them around the circle of players. players.

Ending the Game

The game ends when all players of a single team have crossed from their starting side of the board to the opposite side of the board.

Whichever team gets all of its players across the board to the opposite planet first wins.

There are several circumstances that can cause a team to forefit.
